1. “SAVE CONTACT” BUTTON (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

If someone taps a card and has to manually type your info, you’ve already lost.

Feature:

One button → downloads contact as a .vcf file
Instantly saves:
name
phone
email
business

Why it matters:
This turns your page from “links” into a real business card replacement.

2. TAP HISTORY (YOUR SECRET WEAPON)

When someone taps your card:

log it
show user: “You were tapped by 12 people today”

Optional:

show approximate location (city-level, not creepy)
timestamp

Why it matters:
People love feedback. It makes the product feel alive instead of static.

3. “TAPPED CONNECTIONS”

You said networking. Now actually do it.

Feature:

When two people tap each other → connection created

name
profile
when you met

a digital Rolodex without the awkward “what was their name again?” problem

4. QUICK ACTION BUTTONS (YOU STARTED THIS, FINISH IT)

Let users switch profiles depending on context:

“Business Mode”
“Creative Mode”
“Event Mode”

Same account, different layouts/links.

Why it matters:
A photographer at a wedding vs. at a networking event should not be showing the same thing.

peak activity times

Now users can actually optimize their profile, not just stare at stats.
